  • POST /auth/register

  • POST /auth/forgot-password

  • GET /products

  • GET /quotations

  • POST /invoices

Purchase APIs

6. UI Design Guidelines (Inspired by Munim)

  • Sidebar navigation (fixed)
  • Cards with rounded corners, shadows
  • Modals for create/edit actions
  • Use of icons for actions (Lucide or Heroicons)
  • Theme colors: Light with a soft accent (blue or green)
